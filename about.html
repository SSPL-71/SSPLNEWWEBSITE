Our Philosophy

A claim represents the fulfillment of an insurer's promise to their policyholders. While driving sales is critical, ensuring the protection of policyholders' interests is equally paramount. It is the insurer's responsibility to verify that only legitimate claims are paid out.

At Sarvasiddhanta Services Private Limited, we are committed to helping insurers uphold this responsibility. Our expertise in claims investigation services allows us to assist both life and general insurers in safeguarding the interests of policyholders.

With over 18 years of experience in the industry, we are recognized as leaders in providing high-quality services within agreed-upon turnaround times. We are one of the top claims investigation agencies in Karnataka! We handle everything from straightforward document procurement to more complex and in-depth investigations.

To enhance our efficiency and ensure faster turnaround times, we utilize GenTrac, a comprehensive case management and tracking system. GenTrac streamlines our processes, allowing us to manage and assign cases seamlessly, track progress in real-time, and provide timely updates—all while maintaining the high standards of service our clients expect.

Our well-established processes are designed to meet the unique needs of our clients, ensuring professional and knowledgeable service every step of the way.

At Sarvasiddhanta Services Private Limited, we firmly believe in the values of Integrity, Quality, and Professionalism.
