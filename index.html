We Designed & Built GenTrac

GenTrac is a comprehensive app designed & built by us specifically for claim investigation and verification process. With GenTrac we have streamlined our operations and enhanced efficiency across the entire claim investigation lifecycle.

  • Update and manage new cases seamlessly.
  • Assign cases to field staff for on-site verification.
  • Enable field staff to update records directly from the field.
  • Track case progress and status in real-time.
  • Generate detailed reports for updates and case summaries.
  • Streamline billing and invoicing processes.
  • Produce GST filing reports and vendor billing invoice details effortlessly.
