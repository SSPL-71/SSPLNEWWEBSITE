What is TenniScore?

TenniScore is a professional tennis tournament management platform that allows organizers to create tournaments, manage registrations, score matches, and publish live brackets.

Who is TenniScore meant for?

  • Tennis clubs
  • Tennis academies
  • Residential and sports communities
  • Independent tournament organizers

Why is payment required to create a tournament?

TenniScore is a paid tournament management platform. The one-time fee covers secure tournament creation, automated draws, scoring, live brackets, and ongoing platform maintenance.

Do players or viewers need to pay?

No. Only the organizer pays.

What happens if payment succeeds but tournament creation fails?

An unused organizer entitlement remains linked to your email, allowing tournament creation later without paying again.

What payment methods are supported?

India: Razorpay
International: PayPal
